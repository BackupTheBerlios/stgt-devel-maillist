Get

not head/trunk

Then in your kernel source (linux-2.6.16-rc2 should work) apply the 
tgt-scsi-ml8.patch patch. Build that patched kernel. Then in the 
use-scsi-ml dir build those sources against the newly built and patched 
kernel.
